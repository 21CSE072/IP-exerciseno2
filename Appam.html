APPAM

Appam is a type of thin pancake originating from Tamil Nadu.
It is made with fermented rice batter and coconut milk, traditionally cooked in an appachatti, a deep pan similar in shape to a wok.
It is common in the cuisine of Kerala and Tamil Nadu.
Appam are most frequently served for breakfast or dinner, often with a topping such as an egg.
